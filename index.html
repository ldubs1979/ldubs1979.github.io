About Me

Welcome to my online teaching portfolio. Above you will find links to my resume with letters of recommendation included, my teaching philosophy, and classroom management techniques. Explore the website a little more and you will find literacy and math lessons I have created and implemented, as well as a section discussing my research. Additionally, there is a section dedicated to technology and its utilization in the classroom.

I grew up in Geneva, Illinois. In 2010, I graduated from Geneva Community High School. I chose to attend Augustana College to pursue a lifelong dream of becoming a teacher. I chose Augustana College because of the unique community that a small liberal arts college provides as well as for their outstanding Education department. At Augustana, I majored in Elementary Education with a minor in Spanish. I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Elementary Education in the Spring of 2014.

Teaching Experiences

From Learning to Teaching

After graduating from Augustana College, I began my first year as a teacher with the Geneseo Community Unit School District. I taught 1st grade at Millikin Elementary School in Geneseo, Illinois. My class was made up of 23 students (14 boys and 9 girls). During my time teaching first grade, I was responsible for all curricular areas and had many opportunities to use a variety of educational technologies in the classroom.

Student Teaching

During my thirteen week student teaching placement, I was placed at Bridgeview Elementary School in LeClaire, Iowa. My placement was in a full day kindergarten classroom with 17 students (6 boys and 11 girls). During my time student teaching, I was responsible for the planning and execution of lessons in all curricular areas, communication with parents and staff members, and monitoring social and behavioral development.
